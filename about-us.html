About Us

We are second-year Nursing from the University of Southern Philippines Foundation, Department of Nursing. Developed as part of our Nursing Informatics Course, E-Nurse Library: A Digital Guide to Nursing Procedures is a comprehensive and accessible platform aimed at supporting nursing education. It provides structured procedural guides, visual aids, and essential calculators to enhance clinical knowledge and competence.
